 ## Overview
 HostelCare is a comprehensive application designed to streamline the process of managing student complaints and maintenance requests within a hostel environment. This system aims to provide a structured and efficient way for hostel wardens and staff to receive, track, and resolve issues reported by students, ultimately enhancing the living experience.
 
 ## Client Brief
 A hostel warden requires a robust and user-friendly system to effectively manage student complaints, ensuring timely resolution and improved communication.
 
 ## Key Features
 
 ### For Students
 * **Complaint Submission:** Students can easily create new complaints, specifying the category of the issue (e.g., Electricity, Water, Cleanliness, Internet, Other). They can also include their room number, a detailed description, and an optional photo to provide more context.
 * **Complaint Tracking:** Students have a dedicated view to monitor the status of their submitted complaints, which can be: `Submitted`, `In Progress`, `Resolved`, or `Rejected`.
 * **Communication & Updates:** Students can add comments to their own complaints to provide further information or respond to staff queries.
 * **Prioritization:** Students can upvote existing complaints submitted by others to indicate the urgency or widespread nature of an issue, helping the warden prioritize.
 
 ### For Warden/Staff
 * **Centralized Complaint Management:** Wardens and staff can view all complaints in a single, organized interface.
 * **Advanced Filtering:** Complaints can be efficiently filtered by category, current status, and specific block/floor, allowing for quick identification of relevant issues.
 * **Staff Assignment:** Wardens can assign complaints to specific staff members (identified by name or label) for resolution, ensuring accountability.
 * **Status Updates & Resolution Notes:** Staff can update the status of a complaint and add detailed resolution notes, providing a clear history of actions taken.
 
 ### Insights & Analytics
 * **Open Complaint Dashboard:** A clear display of the number of open complaints categorized by their type, offering an immediate overview of current issues.
 * **"Top Issues" Report:** Identifies the most frequent complaint categories over a configurable period (e.g., last 7, 30, or 90 days), highlighting recurring problems.
 * **Automated Archiving (Optional):** Resolved complaints older than a specified duration can be automatically archived to maintain a clean and current complaint list.
